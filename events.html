Events & Activities

The MMBC social calendar is organised in two parts, reflecting the seasonal nature of canal boating

☀️

Summer Programme

During the summer months, the Commodore arranges a combined cruising and social programme of boating activities which are often joined with other club's rallies and events in the North West, Lancashire and Merseyside areas.

Summer Activities Include:

  • Joint rallies with other North West clubs
  • Events in Lancashire and Merseyside areas
  • Cruising socials with non-boating clubs and societies
  • Regular weekend meetups at the clubhouse
  • Canal-side gatherings and barbecues

For cruising information, contact the Commodore:
David Whitehead: 07944 011568

❄️

Winter Programme

During the winter months the MMBC social committee organises a series of indoor social events in the clubhouse on Saturday nights. The recommended clubhouse capacity is 120 people and these social events are very popular.

Winter Events Feature:

  • Professional entertainment
  • Dancing and music
  • Supper included in most events
  • Themed events and celebrations
  • Social gatherings for members and guests

Dancing, professional entertainment and supper is nearly always provided on these occasions.

For social events information, contact the Social Secretary:
Sharron Brown: 07793 199762
Email: social@mmbc.co.uk
