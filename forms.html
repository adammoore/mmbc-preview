Club Rules - Quick Reference (TL;DR)

A brief overview of the key club rules. For complete details, please refer to the full Club Rules document (PDF).

Membership

  • £125 one-time joining fee for new members
  • Annual subscriptions vary by membership type (£5-£75)
  • All members must abide by club rules and regulations
  • Membership can be terminated for breach of rules

Conduct & Behavior

  • Respectful conduct expected at all times
  • No anti-social behavior on club premises or moorings
  • Quiet hours must be observed at moorings
  • Comply with all Canal & River Trust regulations

Moorings & Boats

  • Moorings allocated by the Moorings Officer
  • Boats must be properly maintained and licensed
  • Valid boat safety certificate (BSC) required
  • Mooring fees must be paid annually
  • Keep moorings tidy and clear of obstructions

Clubhouse Usage

  • Members have access during open hours
  • Clubhouse available for private hire
  • Respect club property and facilities
  • Committee approval required for events

Meetings & Governance

  • Annual General Meeting (AGM) held yearly
  • Committee meetings held regularly
  • All members eligible to vote at AGM
  • Committee elected by membership

Safety & Insurance

  • Members responsible for their own insurance
  • Boat insurance is mandatory
  • Report all accidents or incidents promptly
  • Follow all safety regulations and guidelines

Note: This is a summary only. All members must read and comply with the complete Club Rules (PDF). For questions about club rules, contact the Club Secretary.
