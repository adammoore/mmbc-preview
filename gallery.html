Recent Events

Black Tie Gala 2024

On 8 November 2024, MMBC hosted a spectacular Black Tie Gala at our clubhouse in Lydiate, raising ¬£3,231 for the North West Air Ambulance Charity. The evening featured a three-course meal by Four Seasons catering, live entertainment from singer Gemma Louise Doyle, and a formal cheque presentation to NWAA representative Anne.
