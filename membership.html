Membership

Join MMBC and become part of one of the UK's oldest inland waterways communities. We offer several membership categories to suit different needs.

Membership Categories

Full Boating Member

Â£75 per year

Requirement: Must be a boat owner

  • Includes one named Associate Member
  • Full voting rights at club meetings
  • Can be appointed to positions as officers of the Club
  • Only one full member per boat is allowed
  • Access to all club moorings (subject to availability)
  • Participation in all club activities and events

Associate Membership Options

Associate Member

Â£5 per year

Additional to first Associate Member

  • Normally adult members of the family of Full Members
  • Can also be joint owner of a boat with a Full Member
  • Avoids the inconvenience of having to sign in family or other associates to the club premises
  • Full access to club facilities
  • Participation in all social activities

It is desirable for full members to support associate members to avoid sign-in requirements.

Country Member

Â£5 per year

Non boat-owning members

  • For those with an interest in boats and/or canals
  • Enjoy the social side of boating activities
  • Access to club events and facilities
  • Participate in winter social programme
  • Join cruising socials with other clubs and societies

Junior Member

Free under 16

Family members under 16 years

  • Free membership for children under 16
  • Must be family members of other membership categories
  • Can participate in appropriate club activities
  • Not automatically granted Associate membership at age 16
  • Can become Associate Member at 16 upon request of senior member

Full membership will not be granted until two full years probationary membership has elapsed.

Continued membership is dependent upon the new member taking an active part in club activities. If, at the end of the first or second year, the General Committee consider the member has failed to convince them of their willingness to participate, their membership may be refused.

If occupying a club mooring, the member will be required to vacate the mooring immediately. Alternatively, the Committee may order that the member's probationary period be extended.
