Our Moorings

The club has three sets of linear moorings on the Leeds Liverpool Canal, providing convenient access to facilities and local amenities.

Lydiate Mooring at Bridge 17

Lydiate

Main Location

Our main mooring location with moorings on both north and south banks of the clubhouse at bridge number 17, Pilling Lane, Lydiate. The area immediately in front of the clubhouse is kept clear of permanent moorings for the use by the Commodore, visitors and access to the pump-out and refuelling berths.

Location
Bridge 17, Pilling Lane, Lydiate
Position
North and south banks of the clubhouse
Facilities
  • Clubhouse access with function hall and bar
  • Pump-out berth
  • Diesel and gas available for sale with clean facilities
  • Access to electricity and running water for all boats
  • South bank community hut for social gatherings
  • Winding hole for vessel turning
  • Secure car park
Special Features
Reserved area in front of clubhouse for Commodore and visitors. This is our main location with the most comprehensive facilities.

Haskayne

The most rural of the MMBC sites, located adjacent to bridge 22. This tranquil mooring is conveniently close to the Ship Inn on Rosemary Lane, making it a popular spot for members who enjoy both boating and local hospitality.

Location
Bridge 22, Haskayne
Character
The most rural of our three mooring sites
Nearby Amenities
Close to the Ship Inn pub on Rosemary Lane
Facilities
  • Mains water and electricity connections
  • Purpose-built clubhouse with modest kitchen equipment
  • Limited car parking on Rosemary Lane
Contact
Bank Chairman: Bryan Davidson 07545 317140
Berthing Manager: Charles Gibson 07709 599261

Scarisbrick Mooring at Bridge 27a

Scarisbrick

These moorings adjacent to bridge 27a are conveniently located opposite Nellies Indian Restaurant and feature comprehensive facilities including pump-out services and a slipway that may be hired by members for a reduced rate, making it ideal for boat maintenance activities.

Location
Bridge 27a, Scarisbrick (opposite Nellies Indian Restaurant)
Position
Adjacent to bridge with dedicated facilities
Facilities
  • Pump-out services by the bridge
  • Slipway for hire (reduced rate for members)
  • Electricity connections for all boats
  • Clean toilet facilities
  • Community hut for socializing
  • Dedicated secure car park
  • Jet spray service available
Special Services
Member slipway hire at reduced rates for boat maintenance and repairs. Jet spray service available for cleaning.
Contact
Bank Chairman: Chris Saphier 07973 921680
Berthing Manager: Paul Manning 07526 854611
